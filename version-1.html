Symbol Name Description Example
= Equals The left side is equal to the right side 5 = 3 + 2
≠ Not equal to Left side is not equal to right side 7 ≠ 2 + 8
≈ Approximately equal to Shows estimation, approximation or rounding sin(0.01) ≈ 0.01
sin(0.01) is not exactly 0.01
+ Plus / Addition Add two numbers together 4 + 2 = 8
- Minus / Subtraction Take the second number away from the first 7 - 5 = 2
× Times / Multiplication Adds the number to itself a certain amount of times 3 × 4 = 12
* Multiplication Asterisk Multiplication 3 * 4 = 12
⋅ Multiplication Dot Multiplication 3 ⋅ 4 = 12
÷ Division / Obelus How many times a certain number goes into a different number 8 ÷ 4 = 2
/ Division Slash Division 8 / 4 = 2
— Division Line / Fraction Division 8 4 = 2
( ) Parentheses Calculate expression inside it first 8 × (5 - 2) = 24
8 × 3 = 24
> Strict greater than Left value is bigger in value than right value 12 > 9
12 is greater than 9
< Strict less than Left value is lesser in value than right value 7 < 13
7 is less than 13
≥ Greater than or equal to Left value is bigger in value than or equal to the right value 5 ≥ 4
≤ Less than or equal to Left value is smaller in value than or equal to the right value 4 ≤ 5
± Plus or minus Two results – whether you add or subtract 4 ± 7 = 11 or -3
± Minus or plus Two results – whether you subtract or add 4 ± 7 = -3 or 11
mod Modulo Calculated remainer of two numbers when divided 7 mod 2 = 1
When you do 7 ÷ 2 you get a remainder of 1

4 × 4 × 4 × 4 × 4 = 1024

‰ Permille 1‰ = 1 1000 250‰ × 40 = 10
